🔬 What is NutriScan?

NutriScan is an innovative web-based application that combines the power of machine learning with medical expertise to provide comprehensive malnutrition assessment for children. Our platform analyzes multiple indicators including BMI, skin condition, and nail health to deliver accurate nutrition status predictions.

Key Features:

  • Multi-Modal Analysis: Combines anthropometric measurements with visual assessment of skin and nail conditions
  • WHO Standards Integration: All assessments are based on World Health Organization growth standards
  • AI-Powered Predictions: Advanced machine learning models trained on medical datasets
  • Comprehensive Reporting: Detailed reports with actionable recommendations
  • Patient Management: Complete patient tracking and reminder system
  • Analytics Dashboard: Population-level insights and trends

⚙️ How NutriScan Works

📊

Healthcare professionals input patient demographics, measurements, and upload images of skin and nail conditions.
